Adoption Process

Our adoption process is easy and straightforward. Follow the steps below, and you'll hopefully be approved to adopt one of our amazing pets!

  • Complete one of our adoption applications
  • Schedule an interview with one of our counselor's and meet our rescue animals. (Make sure to bring your application!)
  • If you are approved, go ahead and pick out your pet! (There will be an adoption fee based on the animal picked)
